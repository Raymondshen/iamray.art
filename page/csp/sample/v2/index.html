Summary

There are 5 6 places to initiate an invite in Heylo:

  • From an event, currently a “share” button
  • From the members tab, currently “+ Add”
  • From the members screen, currently an empty state with “Invite”
  • From the group tab, currently, “Invite” and “QR” (QR only on mobile)
  • From the event attendance screen.
  • From the group create onboarding flow

When creating a group, the empty state is the most used, followed by the members tab and group tab. When a group is hard activated, the event screen is most commonly used but followed closely from the members and group tab.

Of all users who sent invites in the last 3 months from Heylo, 65% were initiated from the event screen. 16% came from the group tab, and 12% came from the members screen. Total invites sent are similar - 68% came from the event invite screen.

When just focusing on the group creator, in the last 3 months, 33% are from the member screen empty state! 26% are from the members tab “add” button, 23% are from the group tab, and 18% are from events.

Changing button text and icons can result in a large improvement in invites. In a past experiment, moving from a “mail” icon + “invite” to “send” icon + “share” resulted in a 9% increase in button presses and a 21% increase in conversion Experiment: replace “invite” with “share” on events

Goals and non-goals

Goals

By making small changes to the UI of the button as well as the text, we can increase the percentage of invites from Heylo.

Non-goals

Changes to the event screen. Those will be handled separately in 👋Pre-event engagement
