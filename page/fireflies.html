Summary

I was a Designer at Fireflies.ai, leading the design direction for the company’s branding, digital marketing assets, website, and email templates. Before joining the company, it already had an existing brand, product, and UI design. My task was to update and redesign all existing assets into one cohesive identity and create delightful user experiences.

Within the company, I worked closely with the founder, marketing, psychology, and engineering departments throughout my redesigning process to understand current problems and present solutions and ideas to form a better user experience.
