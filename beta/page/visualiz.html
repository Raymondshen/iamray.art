Summary

Overview

Visualiz is a startup company in Texas aimed to change the way small businesses and consumers interact with each other through augmented reality. I started off as an intern at this startup company and helped work on transforming their ideas into a mobile app.

The initial app lacked branding, contrast, structure, and hierarchy leading to poor user experiences. The other challenge for me was understanding how to design augmented reality. Therefore, before I started working on preparing the company branding and mobile app UI, I had to conduct my own personal research, studies, analysis, and understanding of the market and competitors.

As a result, I delivered the final version mockups of the mobile app UI based on research, discussions, feedback, and inspiration over several months.
