Summary

In 2019, I joined HireClub as a designer, focusing on updating the products, website, and mobile app. The challenge was improving the design while keeping the existing look and products. We wanted to enhance the user experience, address branding issues, and build trust.

Through discussions with the team, we aimed to improve the company's products, revenue, and branding. I redesigned internal web pages, creating a new onboarding experience for users. Additionally, I worked on print materials, pitch decks, and digital marketing assets to enhance the overall brand presence.

Web & Mobile

I revamped the onboarding system to work seamlessly on both web and mobile through user research, testing, and analysis. I also redesigned internal components, enhancing the overall structure and design hierarchy.

Digital Ads

Crafted promotional advertisements for the company's social media and events. A key challenge was infusing diversity into the brand, aligning with HireClub's core values.
