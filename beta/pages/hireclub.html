Hireclub

Designer at Hireclub, focused on UI/UX and creating digital and physical marketing materials.

Hireclub provides career coaches with a platform to help job seekers improve salaries or make career shifts. For job seekers, it matches them with the right coach to fit their needs.

To improve the product, we conducted virtual and in-person user interviews to gain deep insights on improvements for both coaches and customers. We also hosted events to gather feedback and better understand areas for growth.

Onboarding

My first major project was improving the onboarding process to increase user retention and growth. Working with the founder and project manager, we conducted in-person and virtual interviews to uncover drop-off points. Through multiple iterations and A/B testing, we refined the flow to deliver the best user experience.

Social & Marketing Graphics

Creating social media and marketing graphics was a major part of my work at Hireclub, collaborating closely with the product manager. A key challenge was ensuring the imagery reflected diversity.

The Instagram graphics highlighted Hireclub’s achievements with a clean, simple visual approach.

For Facebook graphics, we focused on storytelling to promote and market the services Hireclub offers.

Interactive Covid-19 Map

When COVID-19 caused a surge in unemployment, Hireclub saw an opportunity to help job seekers. I was tasked with designing a web app that displayed a live, interactive map of unemployment submissions and allowed users to include their social media and email for easy employer outreach.
