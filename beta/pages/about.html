Raymond Shen

About me

Who am I?

My name is Raymond Shen, and I am a Product Designer based in San Francisco, CA. Graduated with a B.F.A degree in Web Design & New Media.

work

Who do I do?

I am currently a Product Designer @Netflix Party & Designer @HireClub. In the past recent years, I also worked at Fireflies.ai, Marigold Health, and TheNewCrowd. I combine user research, wireframing, prototyping, psychology, & development to transform complex problems into delightful & engaging solutions. With studies in Web Design & New Media, I understand the constraints for both development on the web and can adapt and design solutions around them effectively.

Passion

What am I passionate about?

As a passionate designer, I believe that understanding the importance of psychology, design, development, and users is essential in creating delightful user interfaces and user experiences.

Being a passionate designer, I am always looking out for new design trends, UI/UX design, & modern front-end developments so that I can collaborate with developers to design beautiful products. I am also passionate about products, technology, and designs that create a significant impact on humanity's progression. But most importantly, I love to work with people who strive to make an impact, a change, and a dent in this modern world we live in today.
