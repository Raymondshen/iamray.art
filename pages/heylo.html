Heylo

As Heylo’s founding product designer, I led end-to-end design for the platform—defining its brand identity, building the design system, and shaping the user experience across mobile, desktop, and marketing channels.

Heylo helps community leaders manage groups of any size with tools for payments, event planning, group chats, documentation, and check-ins.

Through virtual and in-person research sessions, we learned how leaders run their communities, identified pain points, and uncovered opportunities to make Heylo more intuitive and impactful for both leaders and members.

Design System

Heylo’s design system was outdated and misaligned with the company’s core values, failing to reflect the platform’s true identity.

Groups

Group leaders and community members were the heart of Heylo’s platform, so we focused on building essential features that improved every community onboarded to the platform.

Group Profile

Group profiles were redesigned to enhance customization and branding, improving how a group’s identity is presented to both members and new visitors.

Leaders & Members Onboarding

The onboarding experience for leaders and members was redesigned to boost engagement, interaction, and communication, making it easier and more enjoyable to host and join events.

Personalization was enhanced for members to meet their specific needs, while the user flow for leaders was redesigned to simplify group and event setup.

Events

Event features were overhauled to simplify access and equip leaders with the tools needed for efficient community events.

Chatroom

Chatrooms were redesigned with a streamlined bottom bar, improved user flow, and leader tools like ‘@’ mentions to enhance member management and engagement.

Ai Image

Event cover creation was streamlined with AI, using prompts designed for inclusivity and diversity to serve every group.

Social Media

Event sharing enabled members to showcase their group, track engagement through statistics, and celebrate their contributions.

App Store

The app store screenshots was refreshed to match the redesigned branding and visuals, highlighting key features to attract new group leaders.

The redesign of Heylo strengthened the platform’s usability and connection opportunities, delivering a seamless experience for both leaders and members.
