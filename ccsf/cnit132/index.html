Teleparty

Teleparty is all about synchronizing videos with friends on Netflix, Hulu, HBO, HBOMax, and Amazon Prime

The service is currently available on Android, Chrome, and Edge.

Services Teleparty supports:

  • Android App
  • Chrome Browser
  • Edge Browser
